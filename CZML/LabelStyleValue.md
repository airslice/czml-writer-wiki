# LabelStyle (value)

The style of a label.

**Type**: string

## Values

* `FILL` - The text of the label is filled, but not outlined.

* `OUTLINE` - The text of the label is outlined, but not filled.

* `FILL_AND_OUTLINE` - The text of the label is both filled and outlined.
