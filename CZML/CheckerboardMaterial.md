# CheckerboardMaterial

A material that fills the surface with a checkerboard pattern.

**Interpolatable**: no

## Properties

**evenColor** - [[Color]]

The even color.

Default: `white`


**oddColor** - [[Color]]

The odd color.

Default: `black`


**repeat** - [[Repeat]]

The number of times the tiles repeat along each axis.

Default: `[2, 2]`
