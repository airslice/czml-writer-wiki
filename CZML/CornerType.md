#CornerType

The style of a corner.

**Interpolatable**: no

##Properties

**CornerType** - [[CornerType]]

The corner style.  Valid values are "ROUNDED", "MITERED", and "BEVELED".


**reference** - [[Reference]]

The corner style specified as a reference to another property.
