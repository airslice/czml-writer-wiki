#LabelStyle

The style of a label.

**Interpolatable**: no

##Properties

**labelStyle** - [[LabelStyleValue]]

The label style.  Valid values are "FILL", "OUTLINE", and "FILL_AND_OUTLINE".


**reference** - [[ReferenceValue]]

The label style specified as a reference to another property.
